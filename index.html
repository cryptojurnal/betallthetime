⚠ risk disclosure
this is a projection tool, not financial advice. all numbers are estimates based on your inputs. real trading results will differ — markets are unpredictable, execution is imperfect, and discipline varies.
works in both leverage and spot mode — but neither predicts what will actually happen.
what is calculated ✓
· compounding returns
· liq% buffer (cross margin)
· trade fees (maker/taker)
· funding fees
· net P&L after fees
what is not calculated ✗
· slippage & spread
· partial fills
· execution delays
· isolated margin liq
· emotional decisions
cross margin only. liq% formula assumes cross margin. isolated margin behaves differently and these numbers won't apply.

the bet is yours. this tool helps you prepare and stress-test your setup — gains and losses from actual trading are entirely your own responsibility.
